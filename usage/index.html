    Usage¶

    Themes¶

    You can set your theme by using clicking this icon in the header section and selecting your theme. You may need to activate the screen / move the mouse to see this.

    Notes¶

    Adding a note¶

    You can add a note by clicking the 'Floating action button' at the bottom of the page. You may need to activate the screen / move the mouse to see this.

    Moving a note¶

    To move a note, just click and drag the note (whilst not editing the note) to wherever you want it on the page.
